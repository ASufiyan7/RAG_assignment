Cancellation & Grievance Policy 
Order Cancellation: Customers can cancel orders directly through the "My Orders" section until the order has been shipped. Cancellation Charges: Per Indian regulations, no cancellation fees are charged if the order is cancelled before shipping. Grievance Redressal: In compliance with the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, any complaints will be acknowledged by our Grievance Officer within 48 hours and resolved within one month.
